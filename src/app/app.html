What is TavernQuill?

TavernQuill is a high-performance, precision architect for SillyTavern V3 character cards. We built it to be the fastest way to refine AI character personalities, lorebooks, and sprites — with zero server interaction.

SillyTavern V3 Spec

Full support for the Spec V3 standard, including depth prompts and advanced technical specifications.

World Info & Lorebooks

Easily manage world info. TavernQuill extracts lorebooks and ghost data directly from PNG cards.

Privacy-First Architecture

Edit your character's identity and soul in a fully local, browser-native environment.

Dialogue & Greetings

Refine your character's voice, dialogue examples, and greetings with zero server lag.

Frequently Asked Questions

Does TavernQuill require an internet connection?

No. TavernQuill is a browser-native local application. Once loaded, it runs entirely on your machine. We do not upload your character data, sprites, or lorebooks to any server.

Is the SillyTavern V3 specification fully supported?

Yes. We support the complete V3 JSON schema, including depth prompts, talkativeness settings, and enhanced metadata fields required for modern LLM roleplay.

How does TavernQuill handle my privacy?

TavernQuill is privacy-first by design. All PNG metadata extraction and embedding happens locally via the browser's Canvas and FileReader APIs. No data ever leaves your device.
